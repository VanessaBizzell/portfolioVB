BunLife Website

Project Design

The brief for this was to create a website to share a hobby with colleagues. I created a clear and simple site to share the highs and lows of keeping pet rabbits along with useful practical information. Further expansion to include links to rabbit rescues will allow more rabbits to find good homes.

Responsive Design

BunLife is a website designed to work responsively. Using tailwindCSS, it's UI operates well on mobile, tablet and PC screens.

Accessibility and SEO

Accessible design has been acheived through careful use of fonts, alt text and colour-contrast. Search engine optimisation has been considered with clear site structure, links and easy to find informational content.
